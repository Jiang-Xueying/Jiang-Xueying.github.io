<span class='anchor' id='about-me'></span>

I am a second year Ph.D. student at <a href="https://www.ntu.edu.sg/s-lab">S-Lab for Advanced Intelligence</a>, <a href="https://www.ntu.edu.sg/">Nanyang Technological University</a>, supervised by Prof. <a href="https://personal.ntu.edu.sg/shijian.lu/index.htm">Lu Shijian</a>. I received my B.S. degree from <a href="https://www.pku.edu.cn/">Peking University</a> in 2022, supervised by Prof. <a href="http://www.vie.group/ttj">Jiang Tingting</a>.

My research interests include computer vision and deep learning, especially in the fields of 3D object detection, domain generalization, and semantic segmentation.
